## PAGE 00006: (2026x Refresh1) Opening SysML v2 resource in MagicLab

- page_id: `304001148`
- space_key: `MLAB`
- source_url: https://docs.nomagic.com/spaces/MLAB/pages/304001148/2026x+Refresh1+Opening+SysML+v2+resource+in+MagicLab

- ancestors: Versions of MagicLab 2026x > (2026x Refresh1) MagicLab Documentation
- labels: []

### NORMALIZED CONTENT

To open a SysML v2 resource in MagicLab

1. Go to the Resources application.
2. To open the latest version in the project trunk :
  1. If [Resource details preferred management](https://docs.nomagic.com/display/MCS/Managing+users+details#Managingusersdetails-Resourcedetailspreferredmanagement) is disabled , click the resource you want to open directly.
  2. Click [ATTACHMENT filename='actions.png'] next to the resource you want to open and select Open in MagicLab . [ATTACHMENT filename='MagicLab_in_Resources.png']
3. To open the latest version of a branch :
  - Open the Resource details.
  - In the Branches card of the Resource details panel. click [ATTACHMENT filename='actions.png'] next to the branch you want to open and select Open in MagicLab . [ATTACHMENT filename='MagicLab_opening_branch.png']
4. To open a historical version:
  - Click View all at the bottom of the History card.
  - Click [ATTACHMENT filename='actions.png'] next to the version you want to open and select Open in MagicLab.

1. Go to the Resources application.
2. To open the latest version in the project trunk :
  1. If [Resource details preferred management](https://docs.nomagic.com/display/MCS/Managing+users+details#Managingusersdetails-Resourcedetailspreferredmanagement) is disabled , click the resource you want to open directly.

  - Open the Resource details.
  - In the Branches card of the Resource details panel. click [ATTACHMENT filename='actions.png'] next to the branch you want to open and select Open in MagicLab . [ATTACHMENT filename='MagicLab_opening_branch.png']
4. To open a historical version:
  - Click View all at the bottom of the History card.
  - Click [ATTACHMENT filename='actions.png'] next to the version you want to open and select Open in MagicLab.
